Our Services

Five-step process

Our established five-step process will make working with us an engaging & rewarding experience.

We deliver exceptional interior design to elevate your space.

STEP ONE Step One is all about asking the right questions, bringing our experience and foresight to the table to get a solid understanding of your operational requirements and setting objectives. Once the design concept is visualised, our expert team will show you a preliminary presentation with samples, colours, photos and perspective sketches to convey our design concepts.
STEP TWO Step Two is all about knocking the design concept out of the park and making sure we have a plan to realize it. Practically, that's about making the numbers work for the budget, applying building codes, and dovetailing our design to your business operations.
STEP THREE Step Three is all about project management and documentation. Construction plans, reflected ceiling plans, elevations, etc. are finalized and documented. We're working with every stakeholder, from mechanical to electrical to architectural to provide a fully co-ordinated construction drawing package.
